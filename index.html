About us

Who we are

Melborne Capital is one of the emerging investment portfolios managers in the nation. In response to our far-reaching investment capabilities, Melborne Capital has been selected by institutional, corporate and net-worth investors to build and manage their investment portfolios.

At Melborne Capital, we place a premium on investment research. We carefully select securities based on our proprietary research, conducted by our team of local and foreign trained professionals. Our commitment to this process means that our clients have their portfolios managed by professionals who have worked some of the best investment corporations in Nigeria and overseas.

Our Services

What we do

INVESTMENT ADVISORY SERVICES

We focuses on counselling clients on the purchase and sale of securities in exchange for money. Our Clients include individuals, families, corporations, and institutional investors with unique needs and objectives.

FOREIGN EXCHANGE CONSULTANT

We are responsible for educating our clients on the products and services available to help them best utilize their available funds and achieve important monetary goals and providing businesses and individuals with sound advice regarding their financial planning and investments.

FOREIGN EXCHANGE CONSULTANT

We buy and sell foreign currencies. We provide first class customer service to international travellers with the buying and selling. We lend our expertise about overseas financial markets to businesses and individuals seeking to invest globally, we make forecasts, provide in-depth analysis of markets and make trade recommendations based on their findings.

INSURANCE CONSULTANT

We work with individuals and businesses to manage different types of insurance policies such as home, vehicle, income, life, professional indemnity and workers’ compensation insurance. As an Insurance consultant, we review individual and company requirements before proposing tailor-made coverage plans also supervise the processing of legitimate insurance payouts.
